// about

I'm a software engineer who lives closer to the backend but refuses to leave the frontend behind. Most of my day is spent somewhere between Next.js, FastAPI, Kafka topics, and a slightly-too-clever Postgres query.

I like problems where scale, correctness and developer experience all have to be true at once — multi-tenant architectures, event-driven pipelines, AI workflows that actually hold up in production. I care about clean abstractions, small blast radii, and systems that are boring in the best way.

Outside of work I play with AI agents and distributed system design, dive into system design deep-dives and architecture write-ups, and build side projects that scratch my own itch — currently a self-healing CI/CD agent (see below). When I'm not at a keyboard I'm probably gaming.
